5. Results & Outputs

    Flood Extent: Large inundation zones detected in central Lahos during monsoon season.

    Drought Zones: Significant rainfall deficits in northern Lahos.

    Population Impact: Approx. 1.2 million people exposed to floods; 0.8 million affected by drought.

    Cropland Loss: 35% of rice fields impacted by floods; 20% affected by drought stress.

6. User Interface & Experience

    Dropdown Menu: Select hazard type (Floods, Droughts, Cropland Loss, Affected People).

    Interactive Map: Visual overlays with intuitive color palettes (blue for floods, red for droughts).

    Charts: Rainfall anomaly time series, population exposure bar charts.

    Summary Panel: Plainâ€‘language explanations for nonâ€‘technical users.

    Reports: Option to export statistics for decisionâ€‘makers.

7. Challenges

    Data Gaps: Limited temporal resolution of SAR data in some regions.

    Threshold Sensitivity: Flood detection thresholds vary by terrain and land cover.

    Validation: Ground truth data for Lahos is limited, requiring proxy validation.

    Cropland Baseline: ESA WorldCover (2020) used as proxy for 2016 cropland distribution.
